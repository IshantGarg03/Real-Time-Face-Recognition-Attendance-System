Help & Support

How to Register

1. Navigate to the Register page.

2. Enter your name in the provided field.

3. Click "Start Registration" and allow camera access.

4. Position your face in front of the camera until registration is complete.

How to Mark Attendance

1. Navigate to the Mark Attendance page.

2. Click "Mark Attendance" and allow camera access.

3. Position your face in front of the camera to record your attendance.

How to View Attendance

1. Navigate to the View Attendance page.

2. Select a person from the dropdown menu.
